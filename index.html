      Welcome to Polimotions! Searching for information on your local, state, and national politicians has never been easier, or more exciting! Polimotions harnesses the power of News API, Bing Search and FacePlusPlus Facial Detection to bring you accurate, up-to-date political information you can trust. Simply type in the name of a politician and watch as the magic unfolds. Polimotions delivers articles, ratings, biographies, and more right to your fingertips. But wait! That’s not all. Putting a fun twist on a usually mundane task, Polimotions also delivers a photograph and an emotional analysis of your searched politician. Don’t get lost in a mountain of information during campaign season. Try it free today!

    • Instructionsarrow_drop_down

      Begin by using the search bar to find images and articles that are relevant to your topic. Scroll through and select an image you would like to analyze, and simply drag that image over to the container. Click 'RUN ANALYSIS' and watch the magic begin! This button will analyze the selected image and tell you the name, age, and emotions of the person in that image. To analyze more images, just click 'CLEAR' and drag a new one over. Have fun!
